Student Butter Chicken

Description

Student Butter Chicken is a simiplified version of the traditional and delicious butter chicken recipe. It is quite easy to make and requires little to no effort. The recipe can also be used to make multiple meals throughout the week requiring less cooking in the week and is quite satisfying

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken Breast(Cubed)
  • 1 Can of Diced Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • 1/2 Brown Onion(Sliced)
  • Greek Yoghurt
  • Curry Powder
  • Paprika
  • Cooking Cream
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Chilli Powder
  • Cooking Oil

Method

  1. Diced Chicken breast into bite sized cubes
  2. Add oil to the chicken breast and add curry powder,paprika, cumin, chilli powder and greek yoghurt
  3. Mix the chicken with the spices (Color should change to a bright orange)
  4. Dice up 1/2 onion
  5. Add cooking oil and tablespoon of butter into a pan and allow the pan to heat up
  6. Add onion into pan and wait till it becomes transcluscent
  7. Add chicken into pan and let it brown
  8. Once chicken browns add diced tomatoes
  9. Add the previously addedd spices and greek yoghurt into the pan
  10. Let it come to a boil and then put on a lid and turn down the heat
  11. Let it simmer for 15 minutes or until it has reached the consistnecy you prefer
  12. After 15 minutes, add cooking cream into the curry
  13. Serve with rice or naan
